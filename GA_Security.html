Our Security Features


At SwiftRemit, your security is our number one priority. We make use of a wide variety of security feautres to protect your personal data as well as your financial information


  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) Encryption
    Our website uses SSL, which protects your personal information from access by malicious actors

  • Fraud Detection
    We use a fraud detection software to monitor all transaction for any suspicious activity. If any suspicious activity occurs, we will immediately take action to protect your data and your account

  • Anti-Money Laundering (AML)
    We have a strict and comprehensive AML policy that works to prevent money laundering. This includes monitoring transaction for suspicious activity and then reporting it to the authorities

  • Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS)
    We are PCI DSS compliant, which means that we have taken steps to optimize the security of your credit/debit card information to prevent against any misuse of your data and information

  • On-site Protection
    Our servers are physically secured in an undisclosed location with on-site security and surveillance to prevent any unauthorized access
